Career Guidance Help

What is Career Path Recommendation?

The Career Path Recommendation feature helps students by suggesting possible career paths after completing 10th grade based on their personal interests, academic strengths, and preferences.

How does the Career Gap Analysis work?

The Career Gap Analysis identifies the skill gap between a user's current abilities and those required for their desired career. It provides a roadmap for acquiring the necessary skills.

What is Personality-Based Career Prediction?

The Personality-Based Career Prediction feature analyzes your personality traits to recommend suitable career paths that align with your natural strengths and preferences.

Why is Career Guidance Important?

Career guidance is crucial for helping individuals make informed decisions about their future, ensuring they choose a path that aligns with their skills, interests, and long-term goals.
